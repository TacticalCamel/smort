INPUT NODES
0: const 1
1: x position
2: y position
3: age
4: random
5: forward direction
6: nearest left/right border
7: nearest up/down border
8: population density nearby
9: population density forward

HIDDEN NODES:
K-...

OUTPUT NODES:
A: stay
B: move x+
C: move x-
D: move y+
E: move y-
F: move random
G: move forward
H: turn left
I: turn right
J: turn around
